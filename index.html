How it works (seven stubborn steps)

Stage 01

Observe

Rohereignisse ohne Interpretation

We record clicks, keys, focus, timing. Not intent. Not opinions.

  • Raw events, ordered.
  • Context captured, not guessed.

Stage 02

Contextualize

Kandidatenaktionen in Relation setzen

We group raw events into candidate actions and attach context and confidence. Still just a hypothesis.

  • Candidates, not conclusions.
  • Signals attached (target, focus, modifiers).

Stage 03

Propose

Begründung und Konfidenz

We propose the next action with a reason and a confidence. If we're unsure, we say so.

  • Rationale tied to observations.
  • Alternatives kept ready.

Stage 04

Replay

Deterministisches Testbett

We replay the proposal deterministically. Same inputs, same checkpoints. No creativity.

  • Deterministic playback.
  • Snapshots at checkpoints.

Stage 05

Validate

Checks, Assertions, Invarianten

We validate with checks and invariants. Result: PASS, FAIL, or NEED_HUMAN. Mostly FAIL at first. That's healthy.

  • Assertions over state, not vibes.
  • Evidence stored for review.

If it can't be validated, it doesn't run unattended.

Stage 06

Confirm

Menschliches Urteil als Label

When uncertainty remains, a human decides: accept, reject, or adjust. Minimal input. Maximum clarity.

  • Minimal input, recorded.
  • Corrections become labels.

Stage 07

Integrate

Strategien aktualisieren

Validated outcomes tune future proposals: rules, thresholds, heuristics. Improvement without amnesia.

  • Thresholds tune over time.
  • Versions retained for audit.
