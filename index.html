How it works

Stage 01

Observe

Rohereignisse ohne Interpretation

Sensors, transcripts, and operator telemetry arrive in a tamper-evident ledger before any inference. Timestamped, ordered, and forensic, this record guards later abstractions.

  • Immutable event ledger with nanosecond resolution.
  • Parallel capture of signal, transcripts, and operator notes.
  • Audit tokens tie every datum to its origin.

Stage 02

Contextualize

Kandidatenaktionen in Relation setzen

Candidate actions are mapped to procedural narratives, temporal markers, and inferred intent. Context frames narrow the field before any proposal is composed.

  • Heuristics surface intent, risk, and dependencies.
  • Temporal alignment keeps interactions within active loops.
  • Confidence envelopes form ahead of proposals.

Stage 03

Propose

Begründung und Konfidenz

Proposals pair a precise claim with structured rationale referencing the observation ledger. Confidence bands accompany every assertion, and alternative hypotheses wait in reserve.

  • Rationales tie to raw evidence and context.
  • Confidence scoring links to past loop performance.
  • Fallback proposals queue for lower certainty.

Stage 04

Replay

Deterministisches Testbett

The proposal traverses the deterministic testbed, replaying every decision point, timing signal, and handshake with the same inputs, layout, and state.

  • Inputs locked and replayed through the deterministic pipeline.
  • Replay logs capture operator states and control handoffs.
  • Outputs are versioned and ready for comparison.

Stage 05

Validate

Checks, Assertions, Invarianten

Assertions and invariants inspect the replay under PASS/FAIL/NEED_HUMAN gates. Every check reports measurement windows and acceptable drift before a label is considered.

  • Checks enforce propositions, latency, and coherency invariants.
  • Failure modes issue immutable evidence for review.
  • Invariant charts track drift to guide thresholds.

Stage 06

Confirm

Menschliches Urteil als Label

Human reviewers accept, adjust, or reject the validated meaning. Their labels become canonical truth tokens feeding the loop.

  • Acceptance metrics boost confidence envelopes.
  • Adjustments introduce alternative heuristics.
  • Rejections emit annotated reports into the audit ledger.

Stage 07

Integrate

Strategien aktualisieren

Confirmed truths seed thresholds, rules, models, and heuristics, aligning the loop with the next wave of validated automation strategies.

  • Thresholds tune to the latest invariants and operator signals.
  • Rules import human-approved labels and notes.
  • Models and heuristics versioned for audit and fallback.
